ABOUT

Hello. I am STARS, author and moderator, and very pleased to meet you here.

I write stories about my thoughts, my living and something interesting.

The blog is hosted by GitHub, Hexo(4.2.1) and NexT(7.8.0) at the beginning of 2020 but has been re-created by many times due to the lack of computer science, especially programming. (Now I write these words with markdown in Visual Studio Code. What experience!) Anyway, I wish this website could bring you nice stories and my technique on computer has to improve.

Why do I have to host this kind of… ugly…, that means designed not well or properly, blog, hitting keys with fingers instead of signing up other sorts of ones? That’s the question and I really don’t know the reason, which may be the too serious inspection, may be a sense of anonimity or may be the freedom that I can build something on my own? I don’t know…

“Time flys very fast.” — Times always change.

COMMENTS

WHAT NEEDS TO KNOW

  • Only RELEVENT comments with RESPECTFUL languages and attitudes are proper for everyone to make here.
  • Even the most critical comments but only MEANINGFUL ones are still welcomed.
  • Comments require moderator approval at any time after submitted.
  • Comments flagged 3 times will NOT be displayed.
  • Comments in Chinese(Simplified and Traditional), English, Japanese are more likely to be successfully approved.

    WHAT IS PROHIBITED.

  • NO PRIVACY.
  • NO SPAM.
  • NO other VIOLATION, not mentioned yet here, to the LAWS and POLICIES in commentors’ locations.
